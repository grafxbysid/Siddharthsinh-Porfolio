My Design Process

A structured yet flexible approach that ensures thoughtful, user-centered solutions.

Discover

I begin by immersing myself in research to understand the problem space, user needs, and business goals. This includes stakeholder interviews, competitive analysis, and user research to establish a solid foundation for the design process.

User Interviews Market Research Competitive Analysis

Define

I synthesize research findings to define clear problem statements and user personas. This phase establishes the strategic direction for the design, identifying key opportunities and constraints to guide the creative process.

User Personas Journey Mapping Problem Definition

Design

I explore multiple design concepts through sketching, wireframing, and prototyping. This iterative process allows me to refine ideas, establish visual direction, and create comprehensive design systems that ensure consistency.

Wireframing UI Design Prototyping

Deliver

I validate designs through user testing and stakeholder feedback, making necessary refinements. The final deliverables include production-ready assets, documentation, and implementation guidance to ensure successful execution.

User Testing Design Handoff Implementation Support
